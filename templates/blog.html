Building the Future of Medical AI

In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever. We developed a symptom-based disease diagnosis web application leveraging Flask and Machine Learning.

The Problem

Many people experience symptoms and want quick answers. Differentiating between various diseases with overlapping symptoms can be challenging. Our goal was to provide a convenient tool for instant insights.

The Solution

Our web app uses a pre-trained machine learning model to predict the most likely disease based on user symptoms. We combine this with comprehensive advice including precautions, medications, and diet recommendations.

Key Technical Features

  • Advanced SVM Model: Trained on curated clinical datasets.
  • Scalable Architecture: Built using Flask for rapid response.
  • User Privacy: Secure session management and data isolation.

Future Outlook 2026

By combining technology and healthcare, we aim to make reliable health information accessible to everyone. This project represents a significant step forward in remote healthcare solutions.
